Know Us Better

To emerge as a centre of excellence producing globally competent and morally sound professionals in the field of Electronics & Communication Engineering. Established in 2007, the Electronics & Communication department is one of the pioneering department of BPIT which has utilized the science of behavior to develop engineers to meet the industry's standard, which continues to be a major growth area both in India and abroad. The students study a wide range of technologies of communication and data transmission, which have become a vital part of everyday life from traditional radio, telegraphy, microwave systems, etc. To the state-of-the-art optical satellite and cellular mobile communication system. Recently, the B.Tech. ECE program got accreditation from the National Board of Accreditation (NBA).
